AllSaints LookBook UI

Branding, Wireframes, UI style guide, mockup, prototypes, speed test/errors

July, 2015 (two weeks)

AllSaints launched new branding page called 'look book' on Amazon.com also have Login and Pay with Amazon on AllSaints.com. I worked on interaction and its visualization for the look book page presented on desktop and mobile. The business strategy on the look book is 'buy overall look at once'. Based on the business view, I designed one time click interaction to select all look at once and after that, user is able to go into item details that are collected. Additionally, due to the success of the implementation and positive customer feedback, we began working on additional ways to bring more customers and better experience in store.
