Store Experience with Amazon

Ideation, system flow, affinity diagrams, wireframe, low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototype, implementation, system test, demo

October, 2015 - November, 2015

Amazon brings VIP customer to the physical retail store with AllSaints: this is the first time that a Prime member will be able to enjoy Prime benefits when shopping offline. Our customers increasingly switch between online and offline as they go through the purchase funnel. With PrimePass, we were able to establish a way to help grow and monetize our share of the transactions made by our most valuable customers even when they are shopping off Amazon (that was still previously only restricted to the online world). Since offline retail market is still valuable, this represents another step to connect online and offline customer/store.
