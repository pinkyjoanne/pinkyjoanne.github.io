A-z Guarantee





Brainstorm, User research, wireframe, UI assets, visual design specification, implementation

November, 2015 - Present

Proposal of New A-z Guarantee experience, Amazon Pay

Statement: Amazon’s A-to-z Guarantee gives buyers confidence in making purchases on Amazon.com or Amazon Pay. It is a step for buyers to resolve issues with merchants when there are problems with an order, and they are unable to resolve with the merchants directly. Buyers can then file a service chargeback or A-to-z claim. While Amazon.com has a chargeback to claim ratio of 60:40, Amazon Pay has an abysmal 99:1 ratio. The potential reasons for a low ratio is buyer awareness of A-to-z policy, ease of filing a claim, ability for support teams to file a claim on behalf of a buyer, and tracking metrics for the buyer experience. We want to raise the bar on the current A-to-z Guarantee buyer experience and improve the chargeback to claim ratio to 85:15 in 2017.
